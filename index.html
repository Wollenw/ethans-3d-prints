FAQ

Common Questions

How do I submit a file or idea?

Use the quote form to attach details; STL uploads are accepted and we can refine designs if needed.

What materials do you use?

We commonly use PLA and PETG; other materials are available on request. Material choice affects strength and finish.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing considers size, material, print time, and design complexity. We provide transparent quotes before production.

Do you offer rush printing?

Yes — ask about rush options when submitting your request.
